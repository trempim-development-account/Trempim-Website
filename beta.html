Join the closed beta

Trempim is currently in a closed beta on Android. We're inviting students to try the app, share rides and tell us what works (and what doesn't). All it takes to register is your email — we'll send you access details and the download link.

Register

Sign up through our short Google Form below. We only ask for your email so we can add you to the tester list and follow up with the download link. Make sure the email you enter is the same one you use for your Google Play account.

  • Register via Google Form

Get the app

Once you receive a confirmation email, you can download Trempim from the Google Play Store:

  • Google Play

How it works

  • 1. Register — submit your email through the Google Form.
  • 2. Get access — we add you as a tester and send the download link.
  • 3. Install — download Trempim from Google Play on your Android device.
  • 4. Share feedback — use the app and tell us what you think.

Who can join?

Any student in Israel with an Android device who wants to find or offer rides. Spots are limited while we're in closed testing.
