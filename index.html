Trempim

A social network that makes hitchhiking simpler, safer and more accessible for students across Israel — find a ride or become a driver and help fellow students get where they need to go.

  • Learn more
  • Join the Beta

Find a ride

Browse hitchhiking opportunities from students heading your way. Set ride alerts for routes you take often and get notified the moment a matching ride is posted — no more standing at the junction hoping.

  • Try it out

Become a driver

Already making the trip? Offer your empty seats to other students. Publish your route, manage upcoming rides and build up a ride history that shows you're a trusted member of the community.

  • Offer a ride

Travel with confidence

Detailed driver and passenger profiles, ride history and in-app notifications give everyone the information they need to travel safely and reliably — while cutting down on travel costs.

  • Why Trempim

Why we built Trempim

Getting around Israel as a student is expensive and often inconvenient. Informal hitchhiking already happens everywhere — we set out to give it a dedicated, organized and safer home.

Simple & accessible

Make hitchhiking simpler and more accessible for every student across Israel.

Dedicated platform

Discover hitchhiking opportunities through one organized, purpose-built platform.

Safe & reliable

Detailed information about drivers and hitchhikers helps keep every trip safe.

Lower travel costs

Help students cut down on travel expenses by sharing rides that are already happening.
