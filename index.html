The journey

Three stops to a better commute.

Every trip on Trempim follows the same simple line — from finding a seat to arriving with people you can trust.

01
For passengers

Find a ride

Browse hitchhiking opportunities from students heading your way. Set ride alerts for routes you take often and get notified the moment a matching ride is posted — no more standing at the junction hoping.

try it out →
02
For drivers

Become a driver

Already making the trip? Offer your empty seats to other students. Publish your route, manage upcoming rides and build a ride history that shows you're a trusted member of the community.

offer a ride →
03
For everyone

Travel with confidence

Detailed driver and passenger profiles, ride history and in-app notifications give everyone the information they need to travel safely and reliably — while cutting down on travel costs.

Why we built it

Hitchhiking already happens. We gave it a home.

Getting around Israel as a student is expensive and often inconvenient. Informal rides happen everywhere — we set out to make them organized, dependable and safer.
